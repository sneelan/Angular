Purpose: programatic validation, reactive form controls, form groups, and form arrays. define the form structure and behavior programmatically using TypeScript, allowing for more control and flexibility compared to template-driven forms

  • ng-pristine: Represents a form control or form element that has not been modified or interacted with by the user.
  • ng-dirty: Represents a form control or form element that has been modified or interacted with by the user.
  • ng-touched, ng-untouched Represents a form control or form element that has been touched or focused by the user.
  • ng-valid: Represents a form control or form element that has passed all validation checks.
  • ng-invalid: Represents a form control or form element that has failed at least one validation check.
  • ng-pending: Represents a form control or form element that is currently undergoing asynchronous validation.
  • ng-submitted: Represents a form that has been submitted.
